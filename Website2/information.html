Tank Guide

1. Acquiring a Tank

Research: You need to research the tank through your faction's tech tree.
Assembly: Once unlocked, tanks can be built at a vehicle factory by gathering the necessary materials (metal, oil, etc.).
Assembly Stations: After gathering the materials, players can assemble tanks at vehicle assembly stations or advanced vehicle workshops.

2. Tank Types

Light Tank: A faster, smaller tank with lower armor but more maneuverable.
Heavy Tank: A slower, more powerful tank with heavy armor and high damage but less mobility.

3. Tank Controls

Movement: Use WASD for tank movement.
W to move forward.
S to move backward.
A and D for turning left and right.

Gunner/Driver:
Left Mouse Button (LMB): Fire the main cannon.
Right Mouse Button (RMB): Aim or zoom in (depending on your role).

Tank Commands:
Q/E to rotate the turret (gunner).
F to toggle between first-person and third-person views (driver or gunner).

4. Tank Roles

Driver: Responsible for moving the tank and positioning it. You can drive it into combat zones, set up ambushes, or retreat when needed.
Gunner: Responsible for firing the main cannon and secondary weapons (like machine guns) at targets.

5. Tank Tactics

Positioning: Tanks work best when positioned strategically. Try to stay behind cover (e.g., hills, buildings) and use terrain to your advantage.
Support: Coordinate with infantry to clear paths or provide cover fire for them.
Hit and Run: Use the tank's mobility to hit enemy positions quickly and then retreat to safety.
Armor: Heavy tanks should lead assaults due to their durability, while light tanks can scout and harass enemies.

6. Tank Vulnerabilities

Anti-Tank Weapons: Tanks are vulnerable to anti-tank grenades, AT guns, and explosive devices. Always be aware of these threats.
Flanks and Rear: The rear and sides of a tank are weaker than the front. Try to face enemies head-on, or use terrain to hide your vulnerable parts.

7. Repairing and Rearming Tanks

Repairs: You can repair damaged tanks at repair stations using resources like scrap metal or other supplies.
Rearming: Resupply your tank with ammunition at supply depots or rearming stations.
