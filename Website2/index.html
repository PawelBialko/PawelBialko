About Foxhole

Foxhole is a massively multiplayer online game that blends real-time strategy with large-scale warfare. Developed by Cliffhanger Games, it immerses players in a persistent, player-driven world where they are tasked with participating in a prolonged, collaborative war effort. Set in a fictional, World War I-inspired universe, Foxhole emphasizes teamwork, resource management, and strategic planning.

In Foxhole, players join one of two factions — typically represented by two warring sides — and engage in large-scale battles. The game is notable for its focus on logistics, with players needing to gather and transport resources, construct fortifications, and support their faction's war effort in a variety of roles. From frontline combat to supporting roles like medics, engineers, or supply drivers, everyone has a vital part to play.

The battlefield in Foxhole is constantly evolving, and the results of every battle have a lasting impact on the world, which is persistent between sessions. Players can fight in massive, open-world environments with a top-down view, and the battles can span multiple days or even weeks. Strategic depth is added by the need for cooperation, coordination, and long-term planning, making every player's actions crucial to the outcome of the war.

In addition to combat, the game also features crafting and base-building systems, allowing players to create weapons, vehicles, and defensive structures. The focus on logistics and resource management makes it a unique blend of both tactical combat and large-scale strategy. Players must work together, organizing supply lines, building infrastructure, and ensuring their forces are well-equipped for the battle ahead.
