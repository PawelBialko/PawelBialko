Infantry Guide

1. Basic Movement

WASD Keys: Move your character around.
Shift: Sprint (use wisely, drains stamina).
C: Crouch (reduces visibility and improves accuracy).
X: Prone (lay down for better cover and accuracy).

2. Combat Essentials

Left Mouse Button (LMB): Fire your weapon.
Right Mouse Button (RMB): Aim down sights for increased accuracy.
R: Reload your weapon.
F: Interact with objects (e.g., doors, vehicles, or supply crates).

3. Weapons

Rifles: Standard infantry weapon, effective at medium range.
SMGs: Great for close-quarters combat with faster rate of fire but less range.
Shotguns: Powerful at close range, excellent for clearing buildings.
Grenades: Use for flushing out enemies or disrupting formations.
Anti-Tank (AT) Weapons: Use these to disable or destroy enemy vehicles.

4. Communication

Voice Commands: Use voice commands (T) for quick communication like "Enemy spotted" or "Need ammo."
Map Markers: Place markers (M) to give teammates visual directions.

5. Tactics

Cover & Concealment: Always move from cover to cover to reduce exposure.
Teamwork: Coordinate with other infantry or vehicles for combined arms tactics.
Flanking: Try to flank enemies rather than engage head-on. Move around the battlefield to catch enemies off guard.

6. Health & Supplies

Medkits: Heal yourself and teammates.
Ammo Crates: Restock your ammo when supplies are low.
Engineer: Engineers can repair vehicles, build fortifications, and lay down traps.
